About Us

Strategy Consulting Group (Private) Limited (“Strategy Consulting Group”) is a financial, transaction, and project advisory firm advising clients in areas of corporate finance and business strategy. Incorporated in Pakistan, Strategy Consulting Group has more than ten years of successful history in advisory and deal execution for public and private clientele, including M&A, project finance, capital and restructuring advisory. This covers Pakistan and includes clients who have invested in and are involved in Pakistan from the Middle East, Europe, America, and the Far East.


In order to provide the highest level of professional services to our clients, our experienced senior personnel focus their attention on only a limited number of transactions at any one time. This helps ensure a decisive competitive advantage for our clients as we work closely with them from project inception through its ultimate completion.


Expanding from the niche in the power and renewable energy sector, Strategy Consulting Group offers comprehensive solutions to its enlarging portfolio within the manufacturing, aviation, oil and gas, telecom, banking, and financial services sectors.


Attributed to our experienced team, our reputation has evolved and continues to grow stronger, as evident by the fact that more than 70% of our clients provide us with repeat business. Discover how we “connect” and “bridge.” We are the factor – Strategy Consulting Group.
